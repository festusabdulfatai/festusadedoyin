Get in Touch

I welcome enquiries from prospective PhD students (particularly those with backgrounds in computer science, mathematics, economics, or engineering), research collaborators working on machine learning applications, and industry partners seeking applied data science and AI expertise.

I am open to consultancy, Knowledge Transfer Partnerships, funded studentship collaborations, and joint research initiatives. My expertise spans machine learning, FinTech AI, digital health, maritime analytics, and applied econometrics.

Academic Enquiries

For prospective PhD students: Please review my publication record and current projects before making contact. Include a brief research proposal (1-2 pages), CV, and academic transcripts in your initial email.

Topics of interest: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, FinTech AI, Anti-Money Laundering, Digital Health, Maritime Analytics, Applied Econometrics, Responsible AI.

Industry Partnerships

For organisations seeking AI/data science expertise: I have extensive experience in industry-academia collaboration, including a ¬£250,000 Knowledge Transfer Partnership and multiple funded studentships with industry partners.

Services available: Consultancy, KTPs, contract research, funded PhD studentships, advisory board membership, guest lectures, and training workshops.

Research Collaboration

For academic collaborators: I am interested in co-authorship opportunities, joint grant applications, and collaborative research projects in my areas of expertise.

Recent collaborations: IEEE conferences, Elsevier journals, Springer Nature, Routledge, industry-led research projects.
